How Scoring Works

Every slot earns WAE — Watcher Adjusted Earnings

Base Formula

Commercial
Weighted mix of box office gross and profitability (gross minus 2.5× budget)
Prestige
Critic score + Oscar nominations + Oscar wins
Slot Score
65% Commercial + 35% Prestige — then slot bonus & multiplier applied
